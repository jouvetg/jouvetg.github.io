About

Prof. Guillaume Jouvet


  • (From 2022) Substitute Professor at the Institute of Earth Surface Dynamics of the University of Lausanne.
  • (2020-2022) Senior researcher in the Department of Geography of the University of Zurich.
  • (2019-2020) Senior researcher in the Autonomous Systems Laboratory at the ETHZ Zürich.
  • (2014-2019) Senior researcher in the Laboratory of Hydraulics, Hydrology and Glaciology at the ETHZ Zürich.
  • (2010-2014) Post-doctoral researcher in the Numerics of Partial Differential Equations group at the Freie Universität Berlin.
  • (2006-2010) Ph.D. in applied mathematics in the MATHICSE at the Ecole Polytechnique Fédérale de Lausanne (EPFL).
  • (2005-2006) Agrégation de mathématiques & M. S. degree at the Université de Franche-Comté, Besançon.
